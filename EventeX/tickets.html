FAQ

Are there any entrance requirements?

To gain entry to the event, an event pass is required. Please bring either your Aadhar card or PAN card for verification purposes. Thank you.

Do you offer non-Alcoholic drinks in the party?

Yes, there will be a variety of non-alcoholic drinks available at the party. Guests can enjoy mocktails, juices, soft drinks, flavored water, iced tea, and lemonade. We've curated a selection of refreshing beverages to ensure everyone can find something they love.

Is there a dress code?

No, there is no specific dress code. However, our theme for this event is the 'White Party Summer Edition,' so we encourage guests to dress accordingly in white attire to embrace the summery vibes and add to the ambiance of the event.

What's your refund policy?

There is no Refund policy Once you Buy the Ticket
